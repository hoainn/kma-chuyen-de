Dynamic threshold T — exponential moving average (EMA)
T₍ₜ₊₁₎ = max(T_min, β·T₍ₜ₎ + (1−β)·A(W)),   β = 0.9  ·  T₀ = p99.5 of benign scores
designed to suppress false positives from benign behavioral drift
This adaptive threshold is the attack surface analysed in Ch. 4–5: a noisy neighbor can inflate T so that a stealthy attack falls below it (evasion).
